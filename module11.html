Gravitational Force Calculator

This site will calculate the gravitational force given the masses and distance between the objects. You can see how it changes by choosing massed and distance (like 1 kg, 1kg, and 1 meter) and writing down what the force is. Then, you can change the masses or distances by doubling them, tripling them, etc., and see how the force changes.
