The Making of Me and You calculator

This is an interactive calculator on BBC.org. When you input your date of birth, gender, height and weight, this calculator will show you personalized data about:

  • The chemical ingredients that make up you, and what your body is worth
  • How many atoms you are made of, and what can be made with them
  • How many fat, blood, skin and brain cells you have
  • etc.
